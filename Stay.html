Stay

Hilton Homewood Suites

We booked a block of rooms at the Homewood Suites in the hopes that everyone traveling can stay together to enjoy the festivities. There are 16 rooms with king beds and 3 with two queens – all rooms also have a queen size sleeper sofa and a full kitchen. Breakfast and dinner is included. We’ll have a shuttle going from the hotel to the venue on Friday and Saturday. The block is for Friday and Saturday night, but the rate is good Thursday and Sunday if folks want to get here early or hang out on Sunday (everyone is welcome at our house!). The rooms are $199 per night and are currently on our parent’s credit card so you won’t need one to book a room. You can Venmo or Zelle (518-321-4256) our parents whatever you are comfortable with.

Other Options

There are other hotels in the area and no doubt Airbnbs. We’d again like to encourage you to stay at the Homewood Suites. We will be staying there, as well as our family and bridal party. The lounge areas are open 24/7 for us to spend time with one another pre or post wedding. Maybe split a room with friends! Maybe have those cool hotel doors so you can go into each other’s rooms!
