Food

We are so excited to share with you that as much as possible of the food served during the weekend will be sourced locally from small growers, cooked by local chefs! We are working with Excelsior Catering to provide some amazing meals to everyone. The folks at Excelsior Catering have been incredibly supportive of our mission to source as much as possible from local, small food producers and have worked with us to produce what we hope you agree is a stellar menu.

More details will be added to the website once we finalize everything! Check back in July!

Friday evening we will be hosting a welcome dinner at the same venue as the wedding, The Mansion at Cedar Hill in Selkirk, NY. We will be having a cookout with meat and vegan grilling options. We will have sides featuring early September veggies.

The wedding reception on Saturday evening will include a cocktail hour with appetizers. We will serve a variety of appetizers, including seasonal veggies (of course) and lumpia (of course!) Dinner will be a special combination of chef-attended stations serving traditional Filipino food and a vegan curry station.

We will be serving beer and wine throughout the evening. We will also have dessert and coffee after dinner.
